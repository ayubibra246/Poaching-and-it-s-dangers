What is poaching?

Poaching is known as illegally hunting or capturing animals. There are different reasons for why people poach. Some examples are:

  • For fashion and design
  • for medicine
  • For religious practices
  • For the fun of hunting
  • To make money
  • food and cuisine
  • Organized crime and trading

    • Poaching causes various amounts of problems for both plants and animals in the wild. These include:

  • Extinction
  • Scarcity
  • Demand in the poaching industry
  • Chain reaction on the food chain
  • Loss of natural beauty

    • The dangers of poaching animals

    In many different countries there are different punishments for poaching but most countries have similar penalties. These include:

  • Prison sentences
  • Fines
  • Hunting licence revocation
  • Forfeiture of property (Firearms,vehicle,etc.)
